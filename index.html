About Us

The Australian AI Society brings together academics, industry professionals, and enthusiasts passionate about artificial intelligence and robotics. We foster collaboration, knowledge sharing, and innovation in the field.

Our diverse community includes PhD researchers, industry experts, and hobbyists, all united by a shared interest in advancing AI technologies and applications in Australia.
